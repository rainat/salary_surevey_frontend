We are currently looking for a
Product Manager - Browser Games (USA) (m/f)
Goodgame Studios is Europes fastest growing gaming company and one of Germany's most successful technology businesses. With more than 900 employees at our headquarters in Hamburg we develop innovative online games which are played by more than 200 million players in 25 languages across more than 200 countries. Our talented, motivated team of experts and newcomers forms the basis of our success. Become a part of the success story and apply now!

As Product Manager - Browser Games (USA) (m/f), you will be responsible for the performance and strategy of one or several of our online games in the US market. This position is open to applicants with professional experience in the mobile and online games industry.

Your Job:
Your goal:

  • Increase pay conversions, user lifetime and ARPPU/ARPU in the US market
  • Define product strategy and feature pipeline in collaboration with the senior management and other product managers
  • Systematic product analyses and reviews of possible measures on the basis of their suitability for monetization underpin your decisions
  • Coordinate implementation of ideas with the project management and game design departments
  • Monitor, scrutinize and continually optimize production processes
