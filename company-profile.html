    About abacus consulting

    Battling entropy and chaos in the gaming world, Havok creates physics-based game engine middleware, such as the Havok Physics Engine, for animators and video game developers in creating an illusion of reality in 3-D games.

    The company's products are also used by special effects departments working on films such as The Matrix. In addition, Havoc developed the composition tools Havoc Behavior, Havoc Cloth, and Havoc Destruction (used to animate characters's behaviors, textiles, and various demises.) Game developer clients include Activision, Electronic Arts, Midway, and Ubisoft.
